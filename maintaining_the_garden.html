Maintaining the Garden

Regular garden maintenance will have your garden looking beautiful all season long.

Follow these steps for a well-kept garden.

  1. Provide water at least every other day. Less watering is required for native "Prairie" gardens.
  2. Remove visible weeds by the root. This needs to be done at least once a week.
  3. Dead head flowering plants at least once a week to encourage new flower buds.
  4. Watch for garden pests and be proactive about their removal (see insect pests and animal pests).
  5. Add fertilizer or compost a few times during the growing season to encourage the best plant nutrition.
  6. Evaluate what plants are growing poorly and consider moving to another more advantageous spot.
  7. Consider laying down grass clippings or wood chips to shade out weeds.
