Types of Compost

The chart below outlines three different types of compost that you can either make yourself, or purchase from a local provider.

Compost Type Pros/Cons

Aerobic

 Air is incorporated into the compost by turning the compost every couple of days. Turning, air and bacteria do most of the breaking down. This process causes less methane gas.

Anaerobic

 No air, no turning. Strong bacteria do most of the breaking down. Causes more methane gas.

Vermicomposting

 Under controlled conditions, worms are introduced and the worms do most of the breaking down. Less methane gass is produced.
