Animal Pests

When choosing plants consider animal control measures in your garden or choose types that are undesirable to wildlife. A gardener must assume that they will want to eat your tasty plants. Here is a list of common animal pests in Minnesota.

Common Minnesota Animal Garden Pests
Deer
Rabbits
Raccoons
Squirrels
Woodchucks
